Frequently Asked Questions

Are these STL files good for Dungeons & Dragons miniatures?

Yes — every model is sized to print at 32mm scale (the D&D 5e and Pathfinder tabletop standard). Each product page suggests a D&D class fit (Bandit Captain CR 2, Orc War Chief, Minotaur, Goristro, etc.) so you can drop them straight into encounters or use them as recurring NPCs.

What do I get when I buy a DnD miniature STL file?

You get a digital .STL file ready to print, plus a print-report JSON with thin-wall scan and overhang analysis. Files are instant-download via your Etsy account. No physical product is shipped.

Are the meshes printable / repaired?

Yes — every STL is pre-processed in Blender: watertight repair, decimated to a clean triangle count, oriented for printing, and scanned for thin walls and overhangs. Each file includes the diagnostic report so you know exactly where to expect tricky areas.

FDM or resin?

Both. Each model is compatible with FDM (PLA/PETG) and resin (LCD/SLA/DLP). Recommended settings: Resin 0.05mm layer · 1.8-3s exposure · light-medium supports. FDM 0.12-0.20mm layer · 10-15% infill · tree supports.

What scale can I print these at?

Each mini comes scaled to ~120mm (D&D Medium) or larger (D&D Large/Huge for bosses and terrain). Common print scales: 32mm tabletop (D&D 5e / Pathfinder), 75mm collectible, 150mm bust, 200mm+ display. Set the scale freely in your slicer.

Are supports included in the STL file?

No — models come without supports so you can tune supports to your printer and slicer. Tree supports from the base work for most figures. Cloaks, hoods, capes and trailing fabric typically need light supports.

Can I sell prints I make from these files?

Yes. The license allows personal use AND physical sale of printed figures. Print and sell at cons, online, or wherever. Redistribution or resale of the digital STL files themselves is NOT permitted.

How big are the files?

Individual STLs are 6-16 MB compressed (instant download). Multi-mini bundles (6-pack warbands) are split into multiple ZIP files because of Etsy's 20MB per-file limit — typically 3-5 ZIPs per bundle, all download instantly.

Are these compatible with Pathfinder, Frostgrave, KoW, OPR?

Yes. Sized for 32mm tabletop, the minis fit any 28-32mm fantasy system: D&D 5e, Pathfinder 2e, Frostgrave, Kings of War, OPR Grimdark Future Firefight, Mordheim, Frostgrave: Ghost Archipelago. Scale up to 75mm+ for display.

Do you take requests for new monsters or NPCs?

Yes — message us on Etsy with what you'd like to see. We add new minis and warband packs regularly. Past requests have shaped the next batch: bandits, orcs, minotaurs, demons all came from buyer asks.
